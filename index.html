  • Neural Timestepper $f(u^{[t]}, \theta) = u^{[t+1]}$
  • Physical (Numerical) time $\mathcal{P}(u^{[t]}) = u^{[t+1]}$
  • Loss $l(\hat{u}, u)$
  • NN main chain $\left \{ u^{[s]} \right \}_{s=0}^t=\left \{ f^s(u^{[0]}, \theta) \right \}_{s=0}^t$

Method Loss - $J(\theta, u^{[0]})$ IC-Jacobian - $\frac{\partial J}{\partial u^{[0]}}$ Parameter Jacobian - $\frac{\partial J}{\partial \theta}$
Supervised
One-Step Supervised $ l \left( f(u^{[0]}), \mathcal{P}(u^{[0]}) \right) $ $ \frac{\partial l}{\partial \hat{u}}\big|_{u^{[1]}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u}\big|_{u^{[0]}} $ $ \frac{\partial l}{\partial \hat{u}}\big|_{u^{[1]}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\big|_{u^{[0]}} $
Two-Step Supervised Final $ l \left( f^2(u^{[0]}), \mathcal{P}^2(u^{[0]}) \right) $ $ \frac{\partial l}{\partial \hat{u}}\big|_{u^{[2]}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u}\big|_{u^{[1]}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u}\big|_{u^{[0]}} $ $ \frac{\partial l}{\partial \hat{u}}\big|_{u^{[2]}} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\big|_{u^{[1]}} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial u}\big|_{u^{[1]}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta}\big|_{u^{[0]}} \right) $
